Who Let the Dogs Out?

By Michael Friesen and Shayla Tran

The project, Who Let the Dogs Meet Up, resolves a problem to bring together all local information to the users regarding breeders, dog parks, and dog walkers that want to own a dog, attend activities to bond with their dogs, or would like a person to care for their dog while they are away at work. This website allows for a record of dogs to be stored and associated with owners that allows them to see the potential dog walkers, dog meetups, or dog breeders that are available in their area. This database was deemed necessary to store the many different kinds and breeds of dogs that one or more owners may have, and to store all sorts of information necessary for an active community of dog owners.
